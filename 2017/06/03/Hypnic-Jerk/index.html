Hypnic Jerk

I’ve always thought about blogging. The idea just never stuck because I didn’t think I was a good enough writer. I was the kid that brought a pillow to school so I could nap comfortably during AP Lit. Even to this day, I still remember sitting in my chair, slightly drifting in and out of consciousness… awaking suddenly to that sudden jolt of falling. Have you ever wondered why that happens? Hypnic jerk–the involuntary twitch which occurs just as a person is beginning to fall asleep. Even my body was trying to tell me to pay attention in class.

Pilot Post

Well there goes my first attempt, which was surprisingly easier than I thought it would be. The site and everything are kind of rough around the edges right now so bear with me. Things will most likely, actually definitely change, along with more posts being added. The future posts will most likely be about the crazy adventures I do, passionate interests of mine, and anything else on my mind. Anyways whether I plan to use this blog as means of improving my writing or just somewhere to babble away endlessly, hope you guys enjoy what you read!
