Road trip

In this following piece, I’ll actually be talking about something I’ve always thought about doing, but never really knowing when I’d have the chance. I mean how often is it that someone actually takes a cross-country road trip?

Well it just happened to turn out that the perfect chance came up when I graduated from my university in May 2017. To clarify on that, I attended Carnegie Mellon in Pittsburgh PA, and was starting an internship at Samsung Research in bay area California about 2 weeks after my graduation from undergrad. Now instead of flying, I decided that this was the opportunity I was looking for and go all-in on this roadtrip.

This was the general path I decided to take.

I definitely wanted to stop in Chicago first because one of my close college friends who graduated a year prior was working there.

Not to mention there were the added benefits of Lake Michigan, the amazing Chicago architecture, and a good old Chicago-style hot dog!
